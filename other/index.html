About Me:

I’m a recent CS graduate from Boston University. Growing up, I’d spend a lot of time reading what was mostly fiction. I was a big fan of Paolini’s Inheritance Cycle, The Hobbit, and the Lord of the Rings, etc. I wasn’t too big a fan of science fiction, I always preferred reading fantasy. Fiction took up most of what I read until high school, when I came across Plato’s dialogues. If I’m recalling correctly, it was Ion that instilled an interest in philosophy in me (unironically so, since by my lights it’s one of Plato’s worst arguments). From then on, most of my reading was non-fiction, though I still read some fiction, typically wuxia and xianxia novels. I also enjoy reading manga (though I don’t really watch anime). I don’t enjoy playing video games much, with the exceptions of Old School RuneScape, FromSoftware games, Fallout, and the Elder Scrolls Series. To kill time I’ll occasionally solve problems on Project Euler for fun, or tinker with various Linux systems. There was a time when I enjoyed constantly tinkering and recompiling the Linux kernel, though I haven’t done that in a while. Outside of that I enjoy UFC/MMA and weightlifting, the latter I do frequently while the former is something I just watch, really.
